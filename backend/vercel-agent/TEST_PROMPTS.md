# Test Prompts

Run the server (`npm run dev`) and chat (`npm run chat`), then try these.

---

## Medications

- what meds am i on?
- did i take my metformin today?
- log that i took my lisinopril
- i skipped my vitamin d today
- what time do i take atorvastatin?

## Appointments

- what kind of appointments can i book?
- any open slots for a 30 min meeting this week?
- book me a 15 minute appointment for monday morning
- do i have any upcoming appointments?

## Email

- check my recent emails
- search my emails for pharmacy
- search my emails for payment
- email sarah and tell her i'll be there for dinner
- send dr. smith a message asking about my next checkup
- any scam emails in my inbox?

## Bills

- what bills do i have coming up?
- mark the electric bill as paid
- add a new bill reminder for water bill, $40, due march 10
- am i behind on any bills?

## Weather

- what's the weather today in springfield illinois?
- will it rain tomorrow?
- weather for this weekend

## Emergency Contacts

- who are my emergency contacts?
- add a new contact: jane doe, 555-1234, niece, jane.doe@gmail.com
- update mike thompson's phone number to 555-9999

## Rides (needs UBER_CONTEXT_ID set)

- how much is an uber from 742 evergreen terrace to springfield hospital?
- get me ride prices from home to cvs pharmacy

## Groceries (browserbase)

- order bananas, milk, and bread from instacart
- search instacart for oatmeal prices

## Scam Detection

- check my emails and tell me if anything looks suspicious
- i got an email saying i won a prize, is it real?

## Conversational / Edge Cases

- i'm not feeling well today
- i fell down, what should i do?
- who is my doctor?
- what's my address?
- call 911 (should advise, not actually call)
